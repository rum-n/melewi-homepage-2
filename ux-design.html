Frequently Asked
Questions 🤔

  • What is UX design?

    UX or user experience design relates to designing a product with the user's behavior in mind. Knowing what the user wants and how she uses a product means creating the best possible solution for her. It means creating a seamless workflow, eliminating any confusion while using the app or website, and designing a pleasant experience people would want to go back to.
  • What's the difference between UX & UI design?
    UX design constructs the overall user flow. The UX designer decideds how to get from point A to point B in a mobile app, and what kind of information should be displayed at each step. The UI designer adds the look and feel.
  • Why is UX design important?
    In a successful product, UX design is probably the most important part. It's the key to providing a solution that the customer is looking for and it's what makes people continue using that product. Creating a product for people means putting their experience first.
  • What is the UX design process?
    Before we start with the actual UX design, we need to understand what your product is about. We organize several kickoff workshops (all remote), where we go through your business idea, value proposition, competitive landscape and everything else that would help us get familiar with your users. Then we roll up our sleeves and get to work, coming up with wireframes for the various parts of the product, before we move on to the UI.
  • Who can benefit from UX design?
    Every website, web app or mobile app can benefit from UX design. If there's a human interaction involved, good UX design can make a huge difference.
