Frequently Asked
Questions 🤔

  • What is a UX Audit?

    The UX Audit is a practice we use to determine the current state of a product's design. Our team goes through every page of your web or mobile platform and checks every feature, focusing both on usability and visuals. Once that's done, we produce a detailed report with our findings - what we think works and what should be improved.

  • Why is a UX Audit necessary?

    The UX Audit can be used as a point of reference to go back to, when you decide to improve, redesign or add features to your product.Just as every project starts with a plan, it's good to have a summarized analysis of the state of your current design. That way, you know what ot focus on and what the outcome will be.

  • How do you conduct a UX Audit?

    Our team goes through your existing website, web app or mobile app and thoroughly checks every existing functionality. We put ourselves into your users' shoes to spot any inconsistencies that might be confusing or just bad UX practices. Then, we go through our own checklist to see what you have and what's missing. Finally, we put together a report that you can use as a point of reference for future improvements.

  • Who can benefit from a UX Audit?

    The UX Audit is suitable for any company with an existing digital product that feels like something is lacking. You might be experiencing a high churn rate, not attracting as many new users as you'd like or maybe have a high amount of abandoned checkouts. The reason for a bad performing app might be bad UX and this is what the UX Audit can determine.

  • How long does it take to do a UX Audit?

    Our UX Audit usually takes 2 weeks.
